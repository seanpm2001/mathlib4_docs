    Curry and uncurry, as functors. #

    We define curry : ((C × D) ⥤ E) ⥤ (C ⥤ (D ⥤ E)) and uncurry : (C ⥤ (D ⥤ E)) ⥤ ((C × D) ⥤ E), and verify that they provide an equivalence of categories currying : (C ⥤ (D ⥤ E)) ≌ ((C × D) ⥤ E).
