    Oplax natural transformations #

    Just as there are natural transformations between functors, there are oplax natural transformations between oplax functors. The equality in the naturality of natural transformations is replaced by a specified 2-morphism F.map f ‚â´ app b ‚ü∂ app a ‚â´ G.map f in the case of oplax natural transformations.
